Ingredients:
 - 1 cup dry chickpeas
 - 1 tbsp tahini
 - 1 tbsp olive oil
 - 1 tsp dried ground garlic
 - 1 tsp ground cumin
 - salt to taste

Directions:

 1. Soak chickpeas overnight
 2. Cook the chickpeas for around 90 minutes. Strain, preserve cooking water
 3. Process everything in blender or food processor. Add as much cooking water
    as needed to get the consistency you prefer.
 4. Serve with sesame oil and harissa
